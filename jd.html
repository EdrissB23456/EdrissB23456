Job Title: Website Developer

Location: | Remote

Department: IT / Development

Key Responsibilities:

  • Website Development: Design, develop, and maintain websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web development tools.
  • Front-End Development: Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs and optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Back-End Development: Develop server-side logic, define and maintain databases, and ensure high performance and responsiveness.
  • Content Management: Implement and manage CMS platforms such as WordPress, Joomla, or Drupal.
  • SEO and Analytics: Optimize websites for SEO and set up Google Analytics to track and report on website traffic and performance.

Qualifications:

  • Education: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field (preferred but not mandatory).
  • Experience: Proven experience as a Website Developer, with a portfolio of completed projects.
  • Technical Skills:
    • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery.
    • Experience with back-end languages such as PHP, Ruby, Python, or Node.js.
    • Familiarity with database technology such as MySQL, Oracle, and MongoDB.
    • Knowledge of CMS platforms, especially WordPress.

Job Summary

Hot Beans Web is seeking a talented Website Developer to join our development team. This role is crucial for building and maintaining high-quality websites that meet the needs of our clients. The successful candidate will work on a variety of projects, collaborating with designers, project managers, and other developers to deliver outstanding digital solutions.

Experience:

Minimum 3 years of experience in website development. Proven experience with front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). Experience with back-end development and databases is a plus.
